In Memory of Rabbi Eliyahu Fink

Rabbi Eliyahu Fink z”l was more than a rabbi—he was a thinker, a challenger of norms, and a bridge between tradition and the modern world. Through his blog FinkOrSwim and his public writings, he sparked important conversations across Jewish communities and beyond.

This space is being carefully restored as a living archive of his legacy. We are working to bring back his essays, ideas, and reflections for all those who found inspiration in his voice—and for those who never had the chance.

Thank you for your patience as we rebuild this site with the care and respect it deserves.

— The Fink Family & Friends
