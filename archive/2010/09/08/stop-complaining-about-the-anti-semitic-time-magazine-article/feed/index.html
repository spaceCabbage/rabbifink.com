]]> By: Binny http://finkorswim.com/2010/09/08/stop-complaining-about-the-anti-semitic-time-magazine-article/#comment-3089 Wed, 08 Sep 2010 15:48:33 +0000 http://finkorswim.com/?p=2820#comment-3089 I agree with many of the points you made. But while it’s important to read the article and understand its context, you can’t just look past the cover and simply cast it away as “unfortunate.” Covers sell magazines. Covers drive the conversation. TIME knew what it was doing when it put those words on the cover, knowing the ration of people who read covers to people who read articles. It wasn’t an unfortunate cover; it was a deliberate decision, with the financial bottom line in mind, and the collateral damage potentially inflicted ignored. I’m glad that the writer of the article took a measured, fair approach to his reporting and analysis. But while I don’t tend to obsess over anti-Israel bias in the media (mostly because so many others in the Orthodox world overcompensate), it’s fair to call out TIME for mischaracterizing their own article by producing a cover they knew was incendiary.

(As a friend of mine said: it would have been more accurate to title the cover “Why Israelis Don’t Care About Peace.” But that’s not as sensational and won’t sell as many copies.)
