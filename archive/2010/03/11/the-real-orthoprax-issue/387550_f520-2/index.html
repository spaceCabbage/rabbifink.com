This Is My Blog


You have arrived at a place in cyberspace featuring the thoughts, ramblings, musings and Torah of Eliyahu Fink.

You can listen to all my Torah classes on this website by click on "podcasts" above or clicking here.

Come join us at the The Shul on the Beach. You know the Shul on the Venice boardwalk - you've seen it, right? We are there every Friday Night and Saturday Morning for services. We also have classes for all levels of Jewish commitment and observance.

Come check us out! You will feel something special in the air. I guarantee it.
