There are valid exemptions for aliyah, just as there are valid exemptions for men not wearing tefillin this morning.

Unfortunately, in both cases, the vast majority that fail to fulfill the positive commandment do not have a valid exemption.

More tragically, while no shomer mitzvot would ever consider missing a day of tefillin wearing, plenty of shomrei mitzvot obtusely ignore the mitzvah of yishuv haaretz; having been led astray by their chu”l communities with the logic “Everyone else is doing it” (living in chu”l).

We must take the lesson of the spies and use the right glasses to see things the right way.

Indeed.
