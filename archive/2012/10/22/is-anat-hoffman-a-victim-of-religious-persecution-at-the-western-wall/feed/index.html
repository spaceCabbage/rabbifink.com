As R’ Fink observes, there is a difference between a t’shuva/halacha/obligation specifically directed to an individual/organization and a t’shuva regarding enforcement of a halacha by the community. It seems that many people assume that the community and its individuals have a right and obligation to enforce halacha upon the community AND outside the community. Maybe, that is a frequently incorrect assumption.
