If there could ever be a consensus among Orthodox leaders (!) as to how to filter out those that are truly worthy, this would alleviate much of the financial burden that the kollel system places on the rest of the Jewish people (and it might bring Mashiach too!) Funds could be freed up for other causes that are just, and new funds, from the increase in the Orthodox working population, would be available for donation.

This all stems from the massive chasm that exists between the different paths of Orthodox Judaism. Unfortunately, until tolerance is promoted and worlds are united, solutions to the underlying issues stemming from this religious gap will be hard to come by.
