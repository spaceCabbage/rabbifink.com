]]> By: Avraham Bronstein http://finkorswim.com/2012/02/06/the-shabbaton-choir-is-amazing/#comment-7049 Avraham Bronstein Tue, 07 Feb 2012 04:38:00 +0000 http://finkorswim.com/?p=5114#comment-7049 The Ein Kehokeinu is a modern cantorial classic. It was composed by Talmon and was made especially famous in the Great Synagogue of Jerusalem by Naftali Hershtik with his son Netanel (now at The Hampton Synagogue) as child soloist. Rabbi Rosenfeld was a chorister at the Great Synagogue and much of the GS sound is in the Shabbaton experience. Many more of their pieces were composed by Stephen Glass, now the musical director of Shaarei Hashamayaim in Montreal, another great bastion of cantorial/choral music.
