One day after Eliyahu inspires the Jewish crowd to kill 400 priests of Baal he is fleeing for his life with no one to defend him. The 2nd revelation on Mt Sinai (to Eliyahu) shows him that you don’t find Hashem in the fire, or the earthquake, or the mighty gust of wind, but in the ‘still small voice’ (or as Chief Rabbi Jonathan Sacks translates it ‘a sound of thin silence’).
