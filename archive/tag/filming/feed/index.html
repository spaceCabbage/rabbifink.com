Remember this?

It’s been a few months in the making and many hours of discussions and hard work by several people, but mostly Leslie Friedman. We are very pleased to announce that Pacific Jewish Center | The Shul on the Beach will be featured on a full episode of a new series on a very well known cable network. We can’t disclose any more than that right now, but you’ve definitely heard of the network.

If you want to be on the show, you do not need to be a member of our shul, nor do you need to be an orthodox Jew. You can come for filming on Tuesday April 30 and Sunday May 5 to the Shul on the Beach. More specific details will be provided for those who want to attend. You will need to sign a release and you may or may not appear on camera. Tuesday will be a dramatization of a Shabbat service, please dress as you would for a Shabbat service. Sunday will be some sort of party at the shul, please dress appropriately.

Hopefully the show will air in the summer, but anything is possible.

If you want to come please let us know in advance so we can make sure we have the right number of people. You can email me at rabbifink@gmail.com. Thank you.
