Again, I will make my own bias clear: In the past, I have been a volunteer at Planned Parenthood. I support the organization & its mission of enabling all women to make safe, informed, & private decisions about their reproduction. I believe the relatively modest financial support theyy received from the federal government was a good investment in public health.
