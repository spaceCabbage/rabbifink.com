Now What? http://finkorswim.com/2014/07/03/its-over-now-what/ Fri, 06 May 2016 10:55:00 +0000 hourly 1 https://wordpress.org/?v=4.5.2 By: DF http://finkorswim.com/2014/07/03/its-over-now-what/#comment-16559 Mon, 07 Jul 2014 17:18:00 +0000 http://finkorswim.com/?p=8608#comment-16559 The angst you describe over how to continue your new rituals, or not, is typical of all attempts to create new rituals. After 9/11, some groups lit candles or even released balloons on the next anniversary. One year later though, they were gone. And of course, that example can be multiplied.
Perhaps its easier to simply not call them “new rituals.” I see nothing wrong with leaving empty chairs, for example. It’s a nice symbol. But it would never be able to last more than a few weeks without becoming trite. Same with adding a chapter of Psalms at the end of davening. For a few weeks, it has power and meaning. After that, it just becomes another layer of the closing service, and people walk out on it. We Jews already have plenty of rituals, many would say too many, without the need for adding more. A temporary symbol for one Shabbos, or perhaps two, serves the purpose, without attempting to make it anything more than that.
