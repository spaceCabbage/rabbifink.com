Why? What if we begin prayer a little before we become desperate?

The biggest problem with prayer isn’t the question of “Does it work?” or “When to use it?”, but rather the issue of “Why the answer sometimes is ‘no’” What facts, actions, timing, etc cause the answer to be “yes” or “no”? That answer definitely isn’t dependent on level of desperation because it is very clear that even in extremely desperate times, the answer is often “no”. It’s even clear, though less so, that sometimes in less than desperate times, the answer is “yes”.
