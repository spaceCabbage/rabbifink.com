It’s far from a perfect system, as the community is comprised of imperfect human beings, but the basic system is one that encourages challenges. It is an old trope of the religious right that science is just another set of blind beliefs. They use that notion to dismiss evidence of an old earth, or global warming, and anything else that doesn’t conform to their viewpoint. By drawing the analogy you do, you are giving credence to the views of people like that.
