]]> By: Adam Kenigsberg http://finkorswim.com/2013/07/29/morality-out-of-the-mouths-of-babes/#comment-13667 Tue, 30 Jul 2013 10:01:00 +0000 http://finkorswim.com/?p=7414#comment-13667 There is no such thing as altruism

Every choice is a selfish choice.

If I Trust in Hashem, and realize that shmirat hamitzvot will benefit me in this world and the next, that’s a selfish act of yetzer hatov.

If I don’t Trust in Hashem, and try to extract benefit based solely on my own perceptions, that’s a selfish act of yetzer hara.

The lie of altruism only causes depression, causing shomrei mitzvot to despair that they didn’t do a mitzvah “l’shma”.
