We know that some of our ancient sources associate Amalek with a branch of Edom (Esau), one known as Germamia, or possibly Germania.

Perhaps our people are subconsciously sharing this Biblical commandment with the nations of the world.

Obliterate Amalek, and do not forget long enough for (it/him/them) to resurface and gain power.

You shall not forget!
