The only thing that allows a small of parents to “choose” not to vaccinate their children is the fact that the rest of do. It’s called “herd immunity”. You do not need 100% vaccination in order to eliminate the target disease from the population. Those who “choose” not to vaccinate their children (there some children that can’t be vaccinated) should be quarantined to special neighborhoods or islands where nature (and Darwin) can take its course.
