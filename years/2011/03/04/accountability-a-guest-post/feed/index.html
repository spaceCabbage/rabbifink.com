The limud according to the words of the Medrash is that adam tzarich latzeis yedei habrious, kiderech she’hu tzarich latzais Yidei haMakom – one should provide detail to satisfy human curiosity as we are required to be detailed in our spiritual quests. . . this is learned from Moshe in our Parsha. The Midrash continues al achas kama v’kama parnasei hatxzibbur shehein tzrichos latzesi yedei haTzibur.

As such we see the criticism leveled at Moshe, the man who was the ish emunos rav peulos, by the people is what is criticized. However, his providing of an account was lauded, complemented and set as a model we are encouraged to follow. Indeed, we must Always be careful of how we speak about the leaders of our people, but we can humbly and respectfully request they follow the standards Chazal have dictated.
