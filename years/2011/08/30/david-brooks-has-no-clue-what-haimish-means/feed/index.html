1. (in OOT) Is that restaurant heimish?  Heimish = Jewish/Kosher
2. (in Israel) Is that restaurant heimish?  Heimish = Mehadrin Hechsher
3. She’s very heimish.  Heimish = “of good stock” (or if Charedi, she’s willing to work while you learn all day)
4. It’s a heimish apartment building.  Heimish = full of frum Jews.

One time I even heard heimish used as a synonym for frum!

Maybe I’ll think of more later.
